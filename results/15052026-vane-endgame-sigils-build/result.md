## Prerequisites for 18,000+ Power

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Character Level | 100 (max) |
| Weapon | **Treuer Krieger** (Terminus) — fully awakened |
| Masteries | All nodes complete, including Overmasteries |
| Sigils | Tier V or V+ (★★★★★), ideally +15 in all 12 slots |

**Critical threshold:** Terminus weapon passive **Catastrophe** gives +50% ATK and +100% Damage Cap — but only when max HP stays **below 45,000**. Tyranny is required specifically to reduce Vane's naturally high HP under this limit.

## Key Gameplay Notes

- **XXYY combo loop** — Vane's core string. The heavy finisher (YY) hits multiple times, each triggering Hero's Creed for cooldown reduction.
- **Rampart timing** — Use *before* boss AoEs and raid wipes, not reactively. Know the fight patterns.
- **Energy Destruction timing** — Hold until SBA gauge is high for maximum damage scaling.
- **Catastrophe condition** — Always monitor max HP. Any HP-boosting gear can push you over 45,000 and silently disable Catastrophe.

## Corrections & Caveats (from verdict phase)

- **Claude:** Hallucinated some skill names (Rampage, Soul Forge, Arm of Revelation). Vane's actual skills are Rampart, Energy Destruction, Arm Destruction, Drachenstolz.
- **All models:** Exact Hero's Creed cooldown reduction per proc is unconfirmed — percentage vs. flat amount debated.
- **All models:** Supplementary Damage proc behavior (always-on flat vs. percentage-chance per hit) inconsistently described. Community consensus leans toward percentage-chance that scales toward 100% with 2–3 copies.
- **Sigil Booster note:** Terminus weapon adds +1 to each equipped sigil's trait level. Four Damage Cap V sigils = 60 trait levels + 4 from Sigil Booster = 64. Need one more level (sub-trait or wrightstone) to hit the Level 65 cap.
